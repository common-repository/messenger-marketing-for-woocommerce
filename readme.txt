== What is Chatchamp? ==
Chatchamp is a software that helps you communicate with your customers via Messenger. Generate leads via send-to-messenger ads that qualify prospects and are sent directly to your CRM. Retarget customers that leave your site with abandoned cart reminders, shipping updates, order confirmations and more. Increase retention with high open-rate and CTR newsletters, product recommendation quizzes, sales and discount notifications, and more.

== FAQ ==
= What is Messenger Marketing? =
Messenger Marketing is the best marketing strategy to reach and engage e-commerce customers. Having the right Messenger Marketing tool is a must, and chatchamp has made integrating Facebook Messenger into your online store easier than ever. 

= Why Messenger Marketing? =
Messenger Marketing has higher CTR and open rates than email. From generating new leads to reminders and shipping updates; chatchamp helps you communicate with your customers smarter, faster, and more effectively, all through Facebook Messenger.

= How do I install? =
1. Sign up under [chatchamp.com/login](https://app.chatchamp.com/login?utm_source=WooCommerce_Store&utm_medium=Plugin&utm_campaign=WooCommerce_Plugin_Store)
2. Install this WordPress Plugin
3. Fill in your information under
   WooCommerce->Settings->Messenger Marketing

= What are the minimum requirements? =
WooCommerce: 3.0.x or later
WordPress: 4.4 or later
PHP version: 5.5.9 or later

This plugin requires the WooCommerce REST API which is included and enabled by default.
